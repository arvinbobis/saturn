**NVDA trimmed by both:** Coatue reduced NVDA; Atreides reduced 39%. Neither exited — but both rotated capital from NVDA into other names after the Blackwell-driven run-up. Suggests valuation discipline, not thesis change.

**ALAB:** Atreides' ALAB position ($369M, largest add) is the strongest institutional signal for including Astera Labs in the satellite portfolio.
