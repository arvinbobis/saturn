## Active Podcast Watch List

### Acquired (Ben Gilbert & David Rosenthal)
- **Focus:** Deep-dive company histories, often semiconductor/AI infrastructure
- **Why relevant:** Episodes on TSMC, NVIDIA, ASML have become canonical research references
- **Search:** "Acquired podcast [company or theme]"

### Invest Like the Best (Patrick O'Shaughnessy)
- **Focus:** Portfolio manager and founder interviews; often covers AI infrastructure investment themes
- **Why relevant:** Surfaces institutional thinking on supply chain companies before it's consensus
- **Search:** "Invest Like the Best [guest name or company]"

### Odd Lots (Bloomberg — Joe Weisenthal & Tracy Alloway)
- **Focus:** Macro, markets, supply chains
- **Why relevant:** Covers semiconductor supply chain, DRAM cycles, AI CapEx macro in depth
- **Search:** "Odd Lots podcast semiconductor" or "Odd Lots AI chips"

### Eye on AI (Craig S. Smith)
- **Focus:** AI infrastructure, frontier research, enterprise adoption
- **Why relevant:** Surfaces new AI hardware architectures and companies before mainstream coverage
- **Search:** "Eye on AI podcast [topic]"

### Dwarkesh Podcast (Dwarkesh Patel)
- **Focus:** Long-form with AI researchers, founders, economists
- **Why relevant:** Often surfaces compute/hardware bottleneck theses from researchers
- **Search:** "Dwarkesh podcast AI compute"

### The MAD Podcast (Matt Turck)
- **Focus:** Machine learning, AI, data infrastructure
- **Why relevant:** Covers AI infrastructure stack including chips and networking
- **Search:** "MAD podcast AI infrastructure"

### The Knowledge Project (Shane Parrish)
- **Focus:** Mental models, investing frameworks
- **Why relevant:** Occasional deep dives on moat analysis relevant to portfolio thesis
- **Search:** "Knowledge Project semiconductor" or "Knowledge Project AI"
