## Overview

- **Business model:** Pure-play foundry — manufactures chips designed by others; never competes with customers. Revenue = wafer starts × ASP per wafer, by node.
- **Value chain position:** Sits between chip designers (NVIDIA, AMD, Apple, Qualcomm) and end markets. The entire fabless industry depends on TSMC.
- **Founded:** 1987 by Morris Chang. HQ: Hsinchu, Taiwan.
- **Listed:** TWSE: 2330 / NYSE ADR: TSM
- **Scale:** ~65,000+ employees. Fabs in Taiwan, Arizona, Japan, Germany.
- **Market cap:** *(update)*
- **Unique data cadence:** TSMC reports monthly revenue — track between quarterly earnings as leading demand signal.
